Data Science Pay Trends & Predictions

Exploratory analysis and predictive modeling of global Data Science salaries from 2020–2023.

🔍 Project Objectives

  • Evaluate how experience level affects salary in different job roles.
  • Analyze salary predictability based on employee rank and experience.
  • Identify trends across regions and job types.

🛠️ Technologies Used

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy)
  • Data Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn
  • Jupyter Notebook
  • CSV Data Management
  • GitHub Pages (for documentation)

🚀 Key Findings

  • Senior-level positions tend to earn 20–30% more than junior roles across all regions.
  • Data Engineers saw the highest growth in salary predictability based on experience.
  • Western Europe has more predictable salary bands compared to North America.
